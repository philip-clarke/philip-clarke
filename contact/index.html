How to order

Feel free to get in touch as soon as you have a date. We can discuss designs and requirements later on. Usually it is best to book 1 or 2 months in advance to avoid disappointment while the minimum time I would require is 1 week depending on the design.

While many people like to go though my photos on facebook or instagram to get ideas for cakes, you might find a picture on the internet of something different that you want. When I get a picture I try to create the cake as close as possible but with my own style.

For wedding cakes please check out the weddings page.

Cake Size

Cake sizes come in 6, 8, 9, 10 and 12 inches. The following is a rough guide to servings for party servings on a round cake.

  • 6 inch - 10 people
  • 8 inch - 15 people
  • 9 inch - 25 people
  • 10 inch - 35 people
  • 12 inch - 50 people

I would recommend the largest size for a single tier cake to be 9 inch. If you require more cake than this it is better to go for a multi tier cake rather than a larger single tier. Multi tier does not add to the cost.
